FIDEOS

Description

Unos fideitos sin salsa, la polla la dvd

Ingredientes

  • Fideitos
  • Agua
  • Hot

Steps

  1. Put the water into the hot
  2. Hot the water very bien sisis
  3. Put the noodles into the water in the hot
  4. Let the noodles settle for about twelve minutes; don't let them overcook or they'll be slimy (Oughh gross!)
  5. Agarra los fideitos del hot y escurreles el agua till they're not so hot and wet.
  6. Enjoy your fideitos mojados but not that much and hot pero no tanto
