Burnt steak

Description

A pedazo de carne carbonizada, yummy

Ingredients

  • An edible steak
  • Hot
  • Dispensable cooking device
  • Sledgehammer

Steps

  1. Turn on the hot to the maximum till the air starts to get nervous
  2. Put cooking device (a pan, cooking plate, or whatever) into the hot yes yes
  3. Put the edible steak into the cooking device
  4. Let the edible steak sit there for a while. Be careful to not turn around the steak so soon, we want to get the texture of the image above
  5. If you are doubting the steak is undercooked, let it sit there for two or three more hours; you don't want to get poisoned for eating something raw!
  6. When the steak has a healthy void-black colour, it's time to get it out of the used-to-be cooking device! Use your sledgehammer to separate the ingredients Note: at this time you might want to turn on the air extractor
  7. Serve and enjoy!
